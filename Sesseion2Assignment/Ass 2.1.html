Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourie foods.Here is receipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a saptula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
